# Cross-Device Time Tracker --- Product Document

## 1. Product Overview

A personal productivity application that automatically tracks how time
is spent across multiple devices and presents it as a single, unified
timeline. The app helps users understand their digital habits, reduce
distractions, and improve focus without requiring manual timers or
constant input.

The experience is designed to be passive, private, and insightful rather
than intrusive.

## 2. Vision

To become a trusted personal "time mirror" that shows users where their
time actually goes across devices --- clearly, honestly, and without
friction.

## 3. Mission

Help individuals make better decisions about their time by providing
accurate, automatic, and meaningful insights into their digital
behavior.

## 4. Target Users

### Primary Users

- Students
- Professionals
- Developers
- Knowledge workers
- Remote workers
- Productivity enthusiasts

### Secondary Users

- Freelancers tracking work habits
- People trying to reduce screen time
- Individuals preparing for exams
- Anyone seeking digital self-awareness

## 5. Problem Statement

Most people do not accurately remember how they spend their time on
devices. Existing solutions often suffer from one or more of the
following:

- Require manual timers
- Track only a single device
- Provide confusing or overwhelming data
- Lack meaningful insights
- Feel intrusive or privacy-unfriendly
- Encourage guilt rather than improvement

## 6. Value Proposition

- Fully automatic tracking
- Unified view across devices
- Clear, understandable insights
- Minimal user effort required
- Privacy-respecting design
- Focus on improvement, not surveillance

## 7. Core Principles

1.  Automatic over manual\
2.  Insight over raw data\
3.  Non-judgmental design\
4.  User control\
5.  Privacy first

## 8. Key Features

### 8.1 Automatic Activity Tracking

Records active usage across supported devices without manual input.

### 8.2 Unified Timeline

Merges activity into a single chronological view across devices.

### 8.3 Daily Summary

Provides total active time, productivity split, major activities, peak
focus periods, and idle time.

### 8.4 Smart Categorization

Groups activities into categories such as work, learning, communication,
entertainment, and social media.

### 8.5 Productivity Insights

Highlights patterns like productive hours, interruptions, distraction
periods, and deep work sessions.

### 8.6 Cross-Device Sync

Ensures seamless continuity of activity tracking across devices.

### 8.7 Editing & Corrections

Allows users to modify sessions, merge/split entries, reassign
categories, or delete data.

### 8.8 Reports & Trends

Shows daily, weekly, and monthly trends and comparisons.

### 8.9 Notifications & Reminders (Optional)

Gentle summaries, focus nudges, milestones, and periodic reviews.

### 8.10 Privacy Controls

Pause tracking, exclude apps/sites, hide sensitive activity, or delete
history.

## 9. User Experience Goals

The product should feel calm, informative, accurate, effortless, and
respectful of autonomy.
